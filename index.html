Install the Bookmarklet Checker

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar:

Drag Me!

Installation Instructions

  1. Show your bookmarks bar:
    • Chrome: Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac).
    • Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac).
  2. Drag the "Drag Me!" button above to your bookmarks bar.
  3. Use the bookmarklet: Navigate to any webpage and click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.

Troubleshooting

If you're unable to drag the button:

  1. Right-click the "Drag Me!" button.
  2. Select "Bookmark this link" or "Add to Favorites".
  3. Ensure the URL begins with javascript:.
